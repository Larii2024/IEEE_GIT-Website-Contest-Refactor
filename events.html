IEE Events in 2020

Webinar

A webinar on importance & Strategy to prepare for GATE 2021 without any coaching by renowned speaker Mr.Sushant Mutagekar who did his B.Tech KLE MSSCET,Belgaum(2010-2014).He appeared for GATE'14 and was successful enough to score a whopping 99.4 percentile and secured a seat at IIT Madras.

Date 20th June
Website Designing Competition

Students from every branch were allowed to attend the seminar on "What makes a Website look professional" by Prof.Gajendra Deshpande and design the Official Website of IEEE-GIT with submission date 13th August 2020.

AVALANCHE 2020

IEEE Events like Lazer-Mazer,Project Exhibition and Micromatics were among the flagship events this for Avalanche 2020, on 27th,28th and 29th of February.

Udaan 2020
